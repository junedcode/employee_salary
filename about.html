Basic Salary As assigned

DA (Dearness Allowance) Basic × 3%
TA (Travel Allowance) Basic × 3%
PF (Provident Fund) — Deduction Basic × 2%
IT (Income Tax) — Deduction Basic × 2%
Net Salary Basic + HRA + DA + TA − PF − IT
